Directed by : Roar Uthaug

Language : English

Year : 2018

Country : United States

Genre : Action, Adventure, Fantasy

Starring : Alicia Vikander, Dominic West, Walton Goggins, Daniel Wu, Kristin Scott Thomas

Synopsis : Lara Croft (Alicia Vikander) is the fiercely independent daughter of an eccentric adventurer (Dominic West) who vanished years earlier. Hoping to solve the mystery of her father’s disappearance, Croft embarks on a perilous journey to his last-known destination — a fabled tomb on a mythical island that might be somewhere off the coast of Japan, with the help of a drunk sailor (Daniel Wu). The stakes couldn’t be higher as Lara must rely on her sharp mind, blind faith and stubborn spirit to venture into the unknown.

My View :

Tomb Raider is an action, adventure movie directed by the Norwegian director, Roar Uthaug, based on the video game series of the same name and is also a reboot of the Lara Croft film series. This time, Alicia Vikander takes over the role of Lara Croft from Angelina Jolie, who played the role in the previous movies. Tomb raider is a strictly average movie made for action movie lovers. The movie is just above average in most aspects.

The best thing about the movie is Alicia Vikander’s performance. She was really good as Lara Croft. Her Lara Croft was more humane and grounded compared to Angelina Jolie’s Lara Croft. Vikander’s Croft can sweat, get wounded, and is at times vulnerable, and Vikander has done that really well. Its Alicia’s show all the way. There was a scene in which her character has killed a person for the first time, and she was really able to convey the character’s anger, confusion and bewilderment from the act, showing how unholy it is. Other actors, which includes Dominic West, Walton Goggins, Daniel Wu and Kristin Scott Thomas, were also not bad considering the characters they’ve been given.

The technical part of the movie, which includes direction by Roar Uthaug, cinematography by George Richmond, editing by Stuart Baird & Tom Harrison-Read were also good, but had their flaws. Music by Junkie XL was really good and at times helped in making the movie more enjoyable. Plot wise, the movie has got nothing much new to offer, it is a lot similar to Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade, but with more action, and it is also similar to Tron: Legacy at some places. Also, the motives for the actions of the antagonists were not clear enough.

Tomb Raider is an above average action movie made just for money because of the popularity of the video game series of the same name. It is a watchable movie with a great protagonist and a great performance from Alicia Vikander. The characterisation of the antagonists and relations between the characters were not strong and the motives of the antagonists could’ve been made more clearer. This movie is a strictly a studio movie made for people who loves action movies and just want to relax while watching a movie. This is just my personal opinion. Please let me know of your opinion about the movie, if you’ve watched it, through the comments section.

COD Rating : 3/5
